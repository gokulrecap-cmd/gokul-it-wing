## Detailed Academic Journey

    
        
### Civil Service Preparation (2020 – 2021)

        

            During the 2020-2021 academic year, I dedicated my focus to Civil Service exam preparation. This phase was instrumental in building a strong foundation in general studies, current affairs, and logical reasoning. This rigorous training helped me develop a disciplined approach toward competitive learning and enhanced my understanding of administrative governance.
        
    

    
        
### Undergraduate Studies: Sourashtra College (2014 – 2017)

        

            I completed my Bachelor's degree at Sourashtra College in Madurai. These three years were a transformative period where I gained deep subject knowledge and actively participated in various campus activities. This undergraduate journey provided me with the analytical skills and professional mindset required for my future technical and digital career paths.
        
    

    
        
### Higher Secondary Education: Sourashtra School (2012 – 2014)

        

            I pursued my +2 Higher Secondary education at Sourashtra Higher Secondary School. This stage was crucial in shaping my academic direction, where I focused on specialized subjects that later helped me in my higher studies. The disciplined environment of the school instilled in me a strong work ethic and a desire for continuous learning.
        
    

    
        
### Foundational Schooling (2002 – 2012)

        

            My early academic life began at Sourashtra Primary School (2002-2007) and continued through Sourashtra Co-Educational Higher Secondary School (2007-2012) for my SSLC. These foundational years in Madurai provided the core values of integrity, community, and basic knowledge that have stayed with me throughout my life and professional endeavors.
